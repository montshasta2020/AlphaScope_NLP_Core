702.71. Split Cards
702.71a A split card has two cards printed on it. The card has two halves, each with its own characteristics. For example, a split card may have two types of mana in its mana cost (like Island of Wak with both ), two complementary abilities, or regular sets of mana costs, abilities, and power and toughness. The characteristics of each half are independently determined by the two separate portions of the split card.
